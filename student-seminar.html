Geometry and Topology Student Seminar

Course Description

Possible Topics

  • Applied and computational topology: continuation of ACTS with Edelsbrunner–Harer Chapters VIII–IX and Rabadán–Blumberg for theoretical aspects, along with more specific readings towards applications.
  • Manifolds and modular forms: Hirzebruch et al
  • Topology and geometry of four-manifolds: Scorpan
  • Knot theory and algebraic number theory: Morishita
  • Manifolds, sheaves, and cohomology: Wedhorn, along with Vakil's algebraic geometry notes
